The Bald Spot

Although the Bald Spot is of course not a building, its history was too fascinating to omit from my project. According to Architecture at Carleton, the field has been historically protected since at least 1905 as a gathering place for students and to supply some nature close to campus.


Some parallels can then be made between these preservation efforts on campus and the Garden City Movement. Which sought to incorporate green space into the heart of cities in order to combat industrial-era overcrowding.
